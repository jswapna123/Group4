Our clients made a deal with each other. They'd each choose their top 3 inspiration homes without showing the other. If they seemed to
be on the same page, they'd buy land and build a house. If not, they'd stay where they were and invest in a vacation home. This home was
in both of their top 3, so they got to work and started hunting for land to build their mutual dream home. We met our clients during the
land acquisition phase when they reached out for advice regarding whether the land was suitable to build on. We were honored when we
were hired to design and build the home.

Planning was key for this large-scale project and having clients who fully appreciated and trusted our process helped keep things running
smoothly. We worked closely with them during the design phase, redesigning and fine tuning every space, until we reached the perfect
design. Our custom-tailored process provided a well thought out plan giving us the ability to successfully execute the project when it was
time to build. The result is a gorgeous, Modern Farmhouse-style home, situated atop a hill overlooking picturesque views of the
surrounding farmland and a happy family to enjoy them.

Working with BH Design + Build was an amazing professional
experience. Our family decided to develop a piece of land and
their team met with us on site before we had actually purchased the
land. They helped with planning, design, and provided references
to other professionals where needed. From the first time we met
until the project was finished, we felt like BH was part of our
family. The project turned out beautiful and we could not be more
grateful for our decision to work with BH Design + Build!

After starting the design process with another construction company who was not working with their budget in mind, the clients
contacted us for a fresh start. Working quickly to recover precious time lost, our design team was able to create a design plan in line with
the client’s vision and their budget.


The original plan to transform the existing structure was reevaluated after concern regarding the discovery of a musty, water-logged
basement. Keeping the existing design, the team went back to work to create a plan to build a brand new structure. This ultimately
allowed us to make the multi-level living space everything the client had envisioned, including higher ceiling heights and a more energy
efficient space.


Our design and production teams worked closely during this project as they executed several unique design elements by incorporating
various construction materials. The finished product is a stunning display of craftsmanship and collaboration among the client and our
design and production teams.
